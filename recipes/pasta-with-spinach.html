Pasta with Spinach

Description

This is a homemade recipe for pasta with spinach in a sweet red sauce. The ingredients may be tweaked to your personal liking.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 5-6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Handful or two of baby spinach
  • Cooked pasta, any kind

Steps

  1. Add garlic and oil to pot and cook on medium-high, stirring until garlic is golden brown.
  2. Turn heat down to medium and pour in crushed tomatoes. Stir garlic into the sauce. Then slowly incorporate balsamic vinegar and brown sugar.
  3. Add in parmesan and a few handfuls of spinach and stir for 5 minutes.
  4. Serve with your pasta of choice.
