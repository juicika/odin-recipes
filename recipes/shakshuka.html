Shakshuka

Description

Shakshuka is a simple and quick North African dish of eggs poached in a spicy stew of tomatoes and pappers that packs a punch. While this is a fairly straightforward recipe, you can add other sorts of things like sausage, kielbasa, feta, or parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 onions, chopped (about 3 cups)
  • 4 red or green bell peppers, chopped
  • 6 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 2 tbsp grated dried lime, optional
  • 6 tbsp sweet paprika
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 8 cups tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp plus 1 tsp sugar
  • 16 large eggs
